Report an Incident


Share your story by filling out this incident form by OCA - Asian Pacific American Advocates. You will be connected to local counseling resources to help you after this experience. "With your consent, volunteer researchers may use your report to study hate incident trends over time."

Send a Resolution


Use this resolution template created by Stop AAPI Hate to engage with your peers, community, and government officials to speak out against the prejudices against AAPI.

Rapid Response Toolkit


This toolkit provides safety guidance and advice for before, during, or after the occurance of hate crimes. Created from a partnership between OCA - Asian Pacific American Advocates, The Asian American Foundation (TAAF), and other civil rights groups.
